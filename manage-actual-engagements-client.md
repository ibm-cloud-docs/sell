# Consuming managed professional services as a client
{: #consume-mps-client}

To start using managed professional services as a client, navigate to the {{site.data.keyword.cloud}} catalog to discover and provision partner-managed offerings. After you select a service, complete the steps for provisioning an instance and confirm the engagement to help ensure a smooth activation of the service.

## Managing resource provisioning
{: #manage-resource-provisioning}

After the pricing is approved, you can publish the service and view it in the {{site.data.keyword.cloud_notm}} catalog.

1. In the {{site.data.keyword.cloud_notm}} console, click **Catalog > Professional services** and search for the service that was created during onboarding.
1. In the **Create** section, select a plan and complete the following steps to create an instance:
    1. Enter a name.
    1. Enter a contact number.
    1. Optionally, enter additional information.
    1. Click **Create**. You are directed to the instance page.
1. In the instance page, the **Engagement status** section displays the request stated as **Pending**. An email notification is sent to the partner for approval.

For more information on using managed professional services as a partner, see [Administering managed professional services as a partner](/docs/sell?topic=sell-consume-mps-partner&interface=ui).

## Requesting to complete the engagement
{: #request-complete-engagement}

As a client, you can request to complete the engagement, which the partner then reviews and approves. After the engagement is completed, you are responsible for deleting the resources from the instance.
